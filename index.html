Millie Dresselhaus

queen of carbon science

The first time I ever heard about Millie Dresselhaus was in a movie theater. An ad came on just before the movie, sponsored by General Electric. The ad invited viewers to imagine a world where people revered great women scientists as celebrities, rather than the seemingly contradictory pop culture celebrities and personalities generally venerated. I considered this, in the otherwise quiet of the theater, and thought how different my life might have been had I paid more attention to women who were great intellectuals. I realized how profound the message was, and when I thought about building a tribute page for this side project, Millie Dresselhaus was the first person to come to mind.
